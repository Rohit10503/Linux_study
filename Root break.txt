you wish to boot into and press the key 'e' on your keyboard.

step2:
On the next screen, you will see the following kernel boot parameters, 
here find the line that starts with kernel= and add the parameter 
rd.break at the end as shown and press Ctrl + x keys.



step 3: 
Press enter

=> mount -o remount,rw /sysroot/
=> mount | grep sysroot
